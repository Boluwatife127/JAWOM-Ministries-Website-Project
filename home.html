TMC' 24

Preaching Jesus The Life and The Soon Coming King

For over 40 years, we’ve built a thriving community that celebrates faith, love, and unity. Here at JAWOM, we believe in lifting up Jesus and sharing His message with everyone, one person at a time, until it reaches many. Whether you're new or returning, you are invited to be part of something greater—a place to grow, connect, and make a difference. Together, let’s walk in faith and transform lives!

We welcome you to the house of JAWOM. We welcome you to the house of the Lord. We are small enough to know you, big enough to contain you. Welcome you to this great family!

Recent Sermon Topic: Doxology

Preacher: Bishop David Bakare

October 21, 2024

"Praise be to God, from whom all blessings flow!" Join us as we explore the power of doxology—the act of giving glory to God in all things. Discover how our lives, words, and actions can reflect God’s majesty and grace. Through gratitude and worship, we’ll learn to cultivate a spirit that exalts Him in every season of life.

Scripture Focus: Psalm 150:6 – "Let everything that has breath praise the Lord."
