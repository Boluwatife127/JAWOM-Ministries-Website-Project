New to JAWOM?

Welcome to JAWOM! We're thrilled to have you join us. JAWOM is a place of growth, faith, and community. Here, you'll find support, guidance, and resources to help you grow spiritually.

Our community is centered on worship, learning, and helping each other grow in our journey of faith. We hold regular gatherings, conventions, and vigils that cater to all age groups and interests.

Explore our events, meet new friends, and become a part of something meaningful.
